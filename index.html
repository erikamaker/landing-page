You wake up in your dungeon cell, already dead. A captive soul of the labyrinthian underworld, your fate is eternal torture at the hands of monstrous demons. Except, you don't belong here, and neither does your sister. Somewhere in the thirteen treacherous levels of Heck, she waits for you. Face your demons, find the family bones, and climb out of perdition. With some sacrifices and a little magick, you may escape.
